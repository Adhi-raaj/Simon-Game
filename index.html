How to play

  1. Press Start (or press any key) to begin.
  2. Watch the sequence of lights and sounds.
  3. Repeat by clicking/tapping the colored tiles in the same order.
  4. Each correct round increases the level; a mistake ends the game.

Use Speed to make the sequence faster. Toggle Sound to mute.

Simple Simon game — works offline, no external files.
